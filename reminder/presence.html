Reminder: God's presence

Set aside for now the question of to what extent God's presence can be experienced in my current spiritual condition. Set aside past failures or future worries.

The bible teaches that God really is present right here, right now. The spirit of God is able to quench unsatisifed desires and overflow to refresh those around us. He is at work all the time, in every place.
